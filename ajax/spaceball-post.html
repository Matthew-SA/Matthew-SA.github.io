Space Ball

Soccer in space with Online Multiplayer!

Full Lobby System

Browse for an open game from the room list - or create your own!

Game Rooms

Players must select their teams before the game can begin.

Online Multiplayer

Play with up to 6 players per room over Socket.io WebSockets.

Dynamic Camera System

A flexible camera system snaps to the player's ship and breaks away when near edge of the arena.

Customization Options

Players can spend currency to customize their ball and ship cosmetics.

Project Details

Space Ball is a online multiplayer game that supports multiple simultaneous game instances and up to 6 players per game instance. Creating or finding games is made easy with an intuitive game room lobby system. Players can log in to record player stats and customize their appearance and game options.
